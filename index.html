Meet Mitch

Your local go-to guy for just about any job around the house or property.

With over 20 years of hands-on experience, I handle everything from construction and renovations to welding, electrical, woodworking, small engine repairs, eavestrough cleaning — and pretty much anything else that needs fixing or building. I focus on small to medium jobs.

What sets me apart is simple: I answer my phone and reply to messages right away, and I can usually get your job booked and completed fast — often within 72 hours. No endless phone tag, no long waiting lists.

Fully insured and WorkSafeBC compliant, I take pride in reliable, quality work and treating every customer like a neighbour. Whether it's a small repair or a bigger project, I'm here to help with a friendly, straightforward approach.
